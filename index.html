Questions

Do I need to install anything? +
Nope — just download and open in any browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge all work), or host it online so you can access it from anywhere. No app store, no account, no login required.
Does it work offline? +
Yes, completely. Once downloaded it works with no internet connection at all. Perfect for low-stimulation work sessions.
Will my data be saved between sessions? +
The current version stores your plan within the session. A Pro update with local browser saving is in development — Pro Bundle buyers get it free when it's ready.
Is this actually designed for ADHD or is it just a label? +
The Brain Dump and Emotional Reset sections are built directly from the ScarLou&Co ADHD Brain Dump & Emotional Reset Planner — a regulation-first tool, not a productivity system. The whole app is designed around the reality that starting is the hardest part.
Can I use it for client work or multiple businesses? +
Yes — you can open multiple copies of the file, one per client or brand. Labelling them by client name in your folder works great.
